Sensors

  Various sensor types are available for depth estimation. A simple monocular camera is the most basic and cost-effective option. Though stereo, LiDAR, and thermal cameras can be applicable in certain situations, a monocular camera is the most accessible. Many modern smartphones carry at minimum, such a monocular camera. In addition to being an accessible and portable device, mobile phones make an ideal candidate for various depth-reliant applications.

Monocular Cameras

Given that we will rely on a monocular camera, the input of which will be the pixels of an image, the pipeline needs to be built around that. The task therefore is to handle calculating a depth map solely from the data of the visible objects in the image.
